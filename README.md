# Genieflow: The "Uber Eats for Brainpower"

**Genieflow** is an open-source, multi-agent orchestration framework designed to bridge the gap between displaced technical talent and the world's need for high-fidelity domain data.

We are building the **"Uber Eats for brainpower"**—a platform where veteran engineers and domain experts can instantly monetize their tacit knowledge, and AI systems can finally access ground-truth, physical-world intuition.

---

## 🚀 The Mission
We are witnessing a tragedy of human potential. As industrial sectors evolve, highly skilled experts—the architects of our physical world—are being displaced. Their decades of "Tacit Knowledge" (non-textbook engineering intuition) is the most critical bottleneck for AGI, yet it remains trapped and lost.

Genieflow provides a frictionless pipeline to:
1. **Empower Experts:** Provide an on-demand, flexible marketplace to monetize complex problem-solving experience instantly.
2. **Fuel the AGI Revolution:** Transform "discarded" expertise into high-precision instruction datasets suitable for frontier AI training.

---

## 🛠 Technical Architecture
Genieflow is built as a modular **Multi-Agent Orchestration System** designed for reliability and scale.

### Core Components:
*   **The FSM Orchestrator:** A centralized controller utilizing a Finite State Machine (FSM) and an append-only Journal. This ensures every transaction and task routing is fully auditable and state-resilient.
*   **Extraction Agents:** Automated systems that convert unstructured, multi-modal user input (voice, chat, diagrams) into standardized, monetizable service schemas.
*   **The Guardian Agent (The Quality Firewall):** Our proprietary verification layer. It performs multi-round self-consistency checks, cross-references engineering principles, and detects hallucinations.
*   **The Abstention Mechanism:** A built-in "safety valve." If the system's confidence is low, the task is automatically flagged for human review rather than being hallucinated, ensuring "AGI-grade" data integrity.
